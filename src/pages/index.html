Bespoke Wrought Iron Gate Design and Manufacture

At our specialised workshop in North Manchester, we design and manufacture bespoke wrought iron gates and fences to a high degree of craftsmanship. Baytree Forge is a small family run business and has been in operation for over forty years. We take great pride in our work, competitive pricing and good old fashioned customer service.

Our gates and fences are designed to last and look good for many years to come. We achieve this by using the best materials and traditional, tried and tested, manufacturing techniques. We finish all our wrought iron gates and fences with two coats of high quality undercoat and a finish of high grade paint or plastic in any choice of colours.

In recent times, automatic installations have become very popular. We have found ourselves specializing in this type of work. We still fit hand operated gates, but if you want an automated installation, you can feel confident that we are going to do a first class job.

We also manufacture bespoke wrought iron products, repair wrought iron gates and fences, manufacture internal and external hand rails, rebuild and repair walls, cater for the building trade and galvanize our products as required.
